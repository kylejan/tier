About

A Cross-Platform for Project Team Management.

The application is aimed to achieve a common platform on which users can management project team. Then users can join in interested group, share ideas and take part in the meeting and schedule.

Techniques involved in tier to help build creative functions.

50% - Tornado in Python
20% - Html5
30% - Jquery
20% - Objective C

Services

A Cross-Platform for Project Team Management.

Management

Tier supplys flexible platform and technical functions to users for purpose of improving the efficiency of team management. Registered users can create fantastic topics to attract like-minded persons. In the meantime, registered tourist can join in any interested projects and take part in the construction.

Sharing

Brainstorming is the most important part in every project. Traditionally teamers should communicate with each other face-to-face or with the help of external social tools. Team construction is the soul of Tier. Tire focuses on supplying a dedicated platform to team for sharing profiles, ideas, schedules or news.

Scheduling

Scheduling always keep huge influence on the production of ideas. In this case, Tier supplys automatical mechanism to fluency the scheduling process. Divided tasks could be assigned to specific person through Tier and Tier can help team leader to monitor the progress of every task.
